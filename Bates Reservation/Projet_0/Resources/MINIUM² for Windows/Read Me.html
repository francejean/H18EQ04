ABOUT MINIUM²

A long time in the making, MINIUM² is the follow-up to my 2004 icon set MINIUM°, which was finally rendered obsolete with the advent of Mac OS X 10.5 with its 512 px icons. I wasn't able to complete the icons in time - 10.6 is already upon us - but I hope is was the worth wait. I had a blast creating the icons and I hope you will have just as much fun using them.

The goal of MINIUM² is to provide a complete system icon replacement that is both minimalistic and beautiful, thus completing the Mac experience.
